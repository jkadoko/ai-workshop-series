## The Path Forward

### Level 1: Personal Productivity (You Are Here)

**Focus:** Using AI tools effectively in your daily work.

**Next actions:**
- Build a library of 5-10 structured prompt templates for your most common tasks
- Create project-level skills for your team's recurring workflows
- Set up MCP connections to the tools you use daily (GitHub, Jira, Confluence)

---

### Level 2: Team Deployment

**Focus:** Scaling agentic workflows from personal use to team-wide adoption.

**Key activities:**
- **Standardize skills across your team** — move from user-level to project-level skills
- **Establish a shared prompt library** — version-controlled prompt templates in your team's repository
- **Define review protocols** — who reviews AI-generated outputs, how modifications are tracked
- **Document your AI toolchain** — which models, skills, and MCP servers are approved for use
- **Train your team** — use this workshop as the foundation for internal training

**New skills to learn:**
| Skill | Why | Resources |
|-------|-----|-----------|
| GitHub collaboration | Branch/merge workflows for teams | [GitHub Docs](https://docs.github.com) |
| CI/CD pipelines | Automate testing and deployment | GitHub Actions documentation |
| Docker containers | Reproducible environments beyond `.venv` | [Docker Getting Started](https://docs.docker.com/get-started/) |

---

### Level 3: Enterprise Governance

**Focus:** Building organization-wide AI governance and compliance frameworks.

**Key activities:**
- **Implement enterprise-level skills** — organization-mandated standards that all teams follow
- **Create an AI governance policy** — define approved models, tools, data handling rules
- **Establish a "Center of Excellence"** — a cross-functional team that maintains AI standards
- **Build custom MCP servers** — connect AI to your organization's QMS, LIMS, and ERP systems
- **Implement continuous monitoring** — track model performance, detect drift, and maintain validation

**New skills to learn:**
| Skill | Why | Resources |
|-------|-----|-----------|
| FastMCP (Python) | Build custom MCP servers | [FastMCP Documentation](https://github.com/jlowin/fastmcp) |
| Multi-agent orchestration | Coordinate specialized AI agents | Antigravity CLI subagent documentation |
| AI risk management | Formalize risk assessment for AI tools | ISO 14971, NIST AI RMF |
